OUR CLASSES

Strength Training

Want to reduce body fat, increase lean muscle mass and burn calories more efficiently? Strength training to the rescue! Strength training is a key component of overall health and fitness for everyone.Focused on compound lifts, progressive overload, and building serious strength.

60 mins Intermediate Mon/Wed/Fri

HIIT Conditioning

Burn fat and improve endurance with explosive high-intensity interval sessions. . It maximizes cardiovascular fitness, builds muscular endurance, and boosts your metabolic rate, allowing you to achieve the benefits of a longer, steady-state workout in a fraction of the time

45 mins Advanced Tues/Thurs

Functional Fitness

Improve athletic movement, mobility, coordination, and full-body strength. Trains your muscles to work together in daily life. It uses multi-joint, compound movements like squatting, pushing, and lifting to simulate real-world tasks. This approach boosts balance, core strength, and mobility, significantly reducing your risk of injury both in the gym and at home

50 mins Beginner Daily
